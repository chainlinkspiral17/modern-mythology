## script format

each episode follows the format:

- header (arcana, runtime, host, aired)
- COLD OPEN (the host's nod, identical across all 22)
- ACTS (numbered, with runtime ranges)
- CLOSING CARD (host's tag — `the host does not speak, by choice.`)
- ELICIA'S PRODUCTION NOTES (private; not in episode)
- COMMENT THREAD (selected; canonical commenters FT,
  RES_SOL_VERIFY, lostchild, "—", plus episode-specific)

## tonal calibration

these are scripts of a Lynch-tonal web series. the working rules
the deck enforces on the format:

1. **the host does not speak.** Elicia's silence is the series'
   only stylistic absolute.
2. **the 60 Hz hum runs under every episode** and the **9:09 pm
   cable interference** rises at the diegetic hour of 9:09 PM
   exactly, whether the camera sees a clock or not.
3. **dialogue is sparse and unpunctuated by emphasis.** characters
   say things calmly. silences are held; reaction shots are
   permitted to last.
4. **the chapter does not announce itself.** the foreshadow is
   visible to the attentive and unobtrusive otherwise. background
   signage carries vol6 and vol7 forward.
5. **production notes confess the chapter's seam.** every episode
   ends with Elicia's private acknowledgment of what she made,
   what she took, what she withheld, and what she remains
   uncertain about. this is the deck's honesty register.
6. **comments from the canonical four** (FT, RES_SOL_VERIFY,
   lostchild, "—") appear under every script. each handle's
   voice is stable across episodes.

## reading order suggestion

for first-time readers:
- start at **0 Fool** (gateway, casting note about John Frank)
- read **II Priestess** (the meta-episode, Elicia in costume)
- read **XVII Star** (the un-named figure spine, the deck's
  most disciplined refusal)
- read **VII Chariot** (long-take experimental, vol6 foreshadow)
- read **XV Devil** (ensemble, vol1 Triumvirate cameo, vol7
  Halsey introduction)

these five establish the series' tonal range. the remaining
seventeen will land within it.
