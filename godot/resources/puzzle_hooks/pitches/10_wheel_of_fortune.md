# X — THE WHEEL OF FORTUNE : "Closing Arguments Against Chaos"

POV: Erica Campbell. Houston law office — the Fortress. The
case preparation that the Justice (XI) chapter then takes
into the courtroom.

## Tarot lore in play

- Mirror pair AND adjacency: X + XI = 21 AND X → XI direct
  (rare combination). The Wheel and Justice are both the same
  lawyer's case at two stages — fate's wheel turns, choice is
  declined, scales break.
- Classical X ↔ XI = fate ↔ choice. Modern Mythology renders
  the wheel as the act of file selection: four corners (four
  files), one wheel (one selection per turn). The wheel turns
  every time Erica clicks.
- Historical numbering note: in pre-1909 decks, Strength was
  XI and Justice was VIII. The X-as-Wheel position is the swap
  stabilized — the wheel settled where it now is.

  forward-link puzzle: every Dante call in vol6 should resolve

  partly Daigle knowing he ducked a warning that might have
  saved Antonio.

  third refused entity — alongside Lovers and Judgement. The
  caller whose name Alberto would not write.
- ends with Erica's signature timestamp: 3:47 AM. THE FOOL'S
  HOUR. The Wheel chapter's case prep happens at the same
  hour John is wiping the counter. The deck has two POVs at
  the same clock time, eighteen chapters apart.
