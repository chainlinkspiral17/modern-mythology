RIFFMASTER INSTRUMENTS · play in the browser with the PDP guitar controller (or click/keyboard)

RIFFMASTER SYNTH
Monophonic legato lead. Frets pitch the held note; strum re-attacks; hammer-on / pull-off when you change frets without strumming; whammy = real pitch bend. 8 voice presets.
guitarsynth
RIFFMASTER DRUMS
10 synthesised drum voices on the fret pads. Strum hits the highest held pad with velocity. 16-step sequencer with swing for laying patterns under live play.
guitardrums
RIFFMASTER ARP
Arpeggiator. Hold frets — held notes arpeggiate. Strum cycles direction (up / down / up-down / random). Whammy modulates rate. Six timbre presets.
guitararp
RIFFMASTER THEREMIN
Continuous-pitch instrument. Whammy bends ±1 octave around an octave-fret base. Five voice presets, adjustable vibrato. Strum toggles the voice for sustained drones.
guitartheremin
RIFFMASTER BASS
Dedicated low-end monophonic bass. Sub-oscillator stacked per voice, six presets (Finger / Pick / Slap / Synth / Sub / Growl), velocity from fret pressure, whammy = mute/dampen filter sweep. Default root D2; selectable down to E1.
guitarbass
RIFFMASTER DEMONS
Eight demon micro-motifs from the Magician roster (vagrant / cicada / moth / steamboat / weir / filly / starling / husk) plus the steamboat-finale + full-moon-drone cinematic textures. Frets fire one-shots; LOOP mode round-robins held demons.
guitardemons
RIFFMASTER CHORDS
Progression walker. 10 classic progressions (i-VI-III-VII, ii-V-I, doo-wop, andalusian, cathedral, drone…). Strum plays the current chord and advances to the next; frets jump directly to a step. 5 voicings, 5 voice timbres, whammy = filter brightness.
guitarchords
RIFFMASTER DRONES
Polyphonic harmonic layerer. Each fret toggles a sustained voice (root / 5th / octave / ♭3 / ♭7 / 9th / 11th…). Build up the cathedral by adding voices; strum silences all. Six pad patches; whammy = master lowpass; shimmer slider spreads detune.
guitardrone
RIFFMASTER LOOPER
4-track event recorder. Click a track to arm, strum to record events, click again to commit + start looping. Tracks loop independently with their own timing; shift-click cycles a track's voice (lead / bass / bell / pluck). Right-click stops + clears.
guitarlooper
RIFFMASTER STRINGS
Karplus-Strong physics-modelled plucked string. Sounds like a real guitar / lute / koto / ukulele depending on the preset (six). Each strum plucks the string at the current fret; decay + brightness + body resonance are continuously adjustable. Whammy modulates brightness.
guitarstrings

OTHER INSTRUMENTS · keyboard / mouse / touch

TAROT SYNTH
22-card synth pad with 4 banks (Diner / Cathedral / Riverboat / Pastoral). Multiple play modes (CARD / SCALE / GUITAR), chord mode, 4-lane 16-step sequencer, gallery-card art swap.
synthguitar
AMBIENT SYNTH
Chapter-drone generator. 4 tonal voices + 1 noise-floor layer per preset. Live play OR offline-render to WAV (90-300s loopable). 8 presets seeded to the music catalog.
drone
