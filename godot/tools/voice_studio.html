3 · Export

When you have audio for the lines you want, click Export drop-in zip. The zip contains assets/audio/voice/<scene>/NNN.ogg files + an updated scene JSON. Drop it into your godot/ folder.

How to use: Drop a scene JSON on the left (e.g. vol5_ch0_booth6.json). Each voiceable line gets a row below — pick a method on the left, then record / upload / generate per line. When you're done, hit Export drop-in zip on the left. Unzip it inside your godot/ folder; the game picks the audio up on next run.
