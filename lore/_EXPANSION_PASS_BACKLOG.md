complete through their P2s; this is the EXPANSION ledger — new
content and systems, not polish. Everything here is grounded in

## Ground rules

- **Canon first.** The lattice (studios, people, dates) is fixed.
  Expansions that ADD canon must slot between existing dates and
  must not resolve mysteries the catalog deliberately keeps open
  (1976, the eight-pointed star, 220 Hz).
- **Design doc before build** for anything stick-sized. Waves for
  anything mode-sized. One-variable-tracked-paid-off for anything
  smaller (see _SLOWSTOCK_AUTHORING_PLAYBOOK lessons).
- **Modes ride existing machinery** (Manager Mode and the CP
  choice-event engine are the precedents — E3's Manager Mode
  reused the whole Act 1 room; recruitment reused the spike-event
  engine for one new verb).
