## SAMPLE SCRIPT — "The reverse of Mrs. Romero's photo"

Mode: b — found document. The photo is shown twice on the
chapter (Erica's bench, Anna's desk). The script renders the
BACK of the photo — what Mrs. Romero wrote on it before she
gave it to Anna, who paper-clipped it to her case file copy
and gave that to Erica.

```
[REVERSE SIDE / 4x6 matte photo paper / blue ballpoint /
 written in spanish, then in english below, same hand]

para mi querida hija,
si estas mirando esto sin que yo te lo haya dado, te quiero
y por favor no te enojes con ella. ella hizo lo correcto
dandole esto al abogado. yo se lo di a ella para esa razon.
yo no estoy en la foto. la persona en la foto soy yo, pero
no la persona que vas a recordar cuando me hayas leido esto.
esa esta en otra parte. esa fue la que te enseno a montar
bicicleta. esa esta bien.

[english, same blue pen, slightly shakier hand:]

For Anna, in case you give it to her without me.

I am asking you (Anna) to give this photo to the lawyer if
something happens between now and the verdict. The lawyer is
Erica. She is the one I trust because she is your friend.
That is the only standard I have. I do not know her otherwise.

Do not put this on the lawyer's desk. Put it in the case file
so it makes it to the right table when the table is set up.
She will know what to do.

The PetroTex people will say I was confused about the dates.
I was not. The dates were correct. They are the ones who
moved the dates after I gave the deposition.

The photo is from before. From the year your father did the
boat work. The man behind me in the photo is Alberto. He
worked at the dock. He is still alive. He will not testify
because he cannot. The reasons he cannot are not on the
record. He knows them. So do I.

The other man in the photo, half-cropped, is not someone you
would recognize. I am not naming him here. Erica should not
know his name from me. If he is named at trial, it will not
be by me. It will not be by Anna. It will be by him, or it
will be by the deck, or it will not be.

I am old. I am clear. I am very tired. I am not done.

                                            Marta Romero
                                            (your mother)
                                            4 March
```

The script does:
- gives Mrs. Romero a first name (Marta) and a relational
  identity (Anna's mother — the chapter's relational pivot
  that the dual POV never says out loud)
- canonizes Anna and Erica as already friends before the case
  — Erica is who Mrs. Romero trusts because Mrs. Romero
  trusts her DAUGHTER's trust. The case is a triangulation of
  one woman's trust through two readers.
- introduces a half-cropped third figure in the photo — not
  named, will not be named by Mrs. Romero or Anna. Possible
  alignment with the deck's other un-namings (Alberto's
  23 Feb call, Father Quent's "Carlie" third recipient,
  Anna's pair partner). The deck has multiple withheld names;
  Marta is conscientiously NOT adding to the count.
- canonizes ALBERTO as the dockworker who worked with Anna's
  father. Alberto's call log (X), Alberto's photo half-shadow
  (XI). Two cards independently confirm him; this script
  joins the lines.
- names the chapter's true antagonist mechanism: "the PetroTex
  people will say I was confused about the dates. I was not."
  The chapter is structurally about a verdict whose evidence
  has been moved after the fact.
- ends with the date — 4 March. Marta gives Anna the photo
  before the verdict; the verdict is rendered at XI; the
  shattered scales are post-verdict. The photo predates the
  shatter.
