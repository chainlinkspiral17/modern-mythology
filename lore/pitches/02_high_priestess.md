# II — THE HIGH PRIESTESS : "Exit Through the Gift Shop"

POV: Elicia. The gift shop / archive. Halftone monochrome — the
only card in the deck that isn't full-color, marking her as the
observer/recorder rather than an actor.

## Tarot lore in play

- Mirror pair: II + XIX = 21 → **Priestess ↔ Sun**. Inner light
  vs outer light. She lights her own page; Frank (Sun) is lit by
  the same source from outside.
- Adjacencies: I Magician ↔ II Priestess (active will ↔ passive
  archive; he draws the sigil, she preserves it); II Priestess ↔
  III Empress (archive ↔ body; recorded ↔ lived).
- Fool's Journey position: the Priestess is the threshold the
  Fool passes through into the unconscious. She is who holds
  what the seeker cannot keep.
- Classical pomegranate veil is in the painted card as the
  emboss on her closed leather journal.

## The scene as a single room

The gift shop. Four hot zones: the editing console (left,
waveform monitor + tape deck); the bookshelf (right, two named
volumes); the desk (center, journal + tarot notes + the closed
leather journal with the pomegranate sigil); the window (upper-
left, swampy cypress — Graustark continuity).

Idle motion: the editing waveform scrubs slowly across the
monitor; the lantern flame on the desk holds steady; the dust
in the window light does not move (her room does not breathe).

## Hotspots & discoveries

- **The Anya tapes** (upper right). A stack of VHS labeled with
  one repeated name. Touching opens the tape index. Forward-
  seeded character; her canonical first appearance is on her
  own shelf, recorded by someone who has not yet met her.
- **The compass badge** (lower right). MODERN MYTHOLOGY ·
  NARRATIVE STRUCTURE COMPASS (HIGH PRIESTESS NODE). The
  fourth-wall hook — clicking it can later unlock the COMPASS
  overlay's menu tier.
- **The tarot notes journal** (desk). Personal annotations on
  every Major. Each entry is a lore drop for the corresponding
  card; Frank reads the SUN entry on his own card (XIX).
- **The pomegranate journal** (closed, beside the notes). The
  traditional Priestess veil-pattern. Never opens. The room's
  one refused object.
- **The editing waveform** (computer monitor). Scrub-cipher: the
  waveform is a real audio file with a hidden vocal line that
  surfaces when scrubbed slowly.
- **The shelf books**. OCCULT SYMBOLOGY OF THE ASCENDANT and
  MYSTERY CULT TEXTS. Both spines carry the infinity sigil after
  Magician's iron is touched (cross-card gate, live).

## SAMPLE SCRIPT — "ANYA_03 (partial transcript)"

A document, not a scene. The transcript of one of Elicia's tapes
— Anya, the not-yet-introduced character whose name is already
on three of her shelves. Mode: found document. The painted card
shows the tape; this is what's on it.

```
[ANYA_03 / partial / cassette side B, 14:22 in]
[transcribed e.l. — 03 March, the priestess gift shop]

ANYA: — but you know what I mean about the cousin thing.

E:    I don't.

ANYA: The cousin thing where everybody knows the cousin and
      nobody — like — nobody volunteers what they know about
      the cousin until you say the name first.

E:    That's not — Anya — that's not a tradition, that's gossip.

ANYA: It IS a tradition. It's gossip that has become a tradition
      by being everyone's at once. That's the part nobody — like
      — Catholics don't get this because they only have saints
      and saints are different. Saints you can name.

      [pause, 11 sec.]

ANYA: I'm not Catholic.

E:    I know.

ANYA: I was going to say I'm not Catholic. I forgot you knew.

      [pause, 4 sec.]

ANYA: Are you recording this.

E:    Yes.

ANYA: Why.

E:    Same reason I record the other ones.

ANYA: Which is.

E:    Which is that you don't get to choose what was important
      yet. The cousin thing might be important. I don't know.

ANYA: The cousin thing's not important. The cousin thing is just
      something I — I'm telling you about. It's just a — okay
      whatever. Tape it.

      [pause, 9 sec.]

ANYA: You think the cousin thing is important.

E:    I think you brought it up twice. Once in the kitchen,
      once now. You haven't said the cousin's name.

ANYA: Why would I say his name.

E:    See.

      [pause, 6 sec.]

ANYA: It's not a HIS. Whatever you think — it's not a — okay.
      Okay. Tape this part too I guess. I'm leaving. Don't —

      [recording ends]

[notes, e.l.: 'cousin' referenced in tapes 03, 07, 11. Anya
has never used a name or pronoun for this person. Possibly
diegetic; possibly a placeholder she is using for someone she
will not name to me. To investigate: who in graustark would
not be safely nameable on tape in this room — i.e., who would
she suspect this tape might reach.]
```

The transcript hints at:
- Anya's tone, which the deck has only implied (not shown)
- A "cousin" Anya refuses to name — a sixth-degree forward seed
  for whoever lands as Anya's vol6 or vol7 partner
- Elicia's working method: she records, she annotates, she does
  not press the question; she lets the gap that the speaker is
  protecting be its own data
- The Anya tapes as a corpus, not a single object — three of
  twenty-something tapes carry the "cousin" reference
