# XIX — THE SUN : "Pattern Recognition in Dust Motes"

POV: Frank. The same observation chamber from Temperance,
Saturday morning, light direct. Frank's second chapter; the
pattern he disciplined himself not to write on Tuesday is
finally written here.

## Tarot lore in play

- Mirror pair: XIX + II = 21 → **Sun ↔ Priestess**. Outer light
  ↔ inner light. The Priestess archives every reader's
  marginalia; Frank reads her annotation of his own chapter.
- Adjacencies: XVIII Moon ↔ XIX Sun (illusion → clarity); XIX
  Sun ↔ XX Judgement (clarity → awakening). Night trio
  closes.
- Classical Sun = clarity, joy, the child on the horse. Modern
  Mythology refuses the child — Frank is the figure, dust is
  the sun's text.
