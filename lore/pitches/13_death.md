POV: none rendered directly. The ward is the chapter. The

## Tarot lore in play

- Mirror pair: XIII + VIII = 21 → **Death ↔ Strength**. Hard
  ending ↔ soft mastery. Same gentle hand at two temperatures
  — gloved at XIII, bare at VIII.
- Adjacencies: XII Hanged Man ↔ XIII Death (suspension →
  transformation); XIII Death ↔ XIV Temperance (transit →
  moderation).
- Classical Death = transformation, the great equalizer,
  the white-horse skeleton crossing fields of fallen kings.
  Modern Mythology refuses every traditional motif. No
  skeleton, no scythe, no horse. Death is six beds, two
  empty, a gloved hand on a wrist.
